About This App

The Reset Compass is a free tool by Evolution of Wellness to help you reset your mental and physical state fast — wherever you are.

Use it to take back control of your energy and momentum, one step at a time.

🚀 Growing

Choose bold actions that move you forward with purpose.

  • Start a meaningful project
  • Make that call you've been putting off
  • Ask for feedback
  • Step out of your comfort zone

🌿 Grounded

Stay centered and calm by doing something restorative.

  • Meditate or pray
  • Do light stretching
  • Listen to calming music
  • Step outside into sunlight

🧭 Drifting

Re-anchor your attention and get back on track.

  • Clear off your desk
  • Write a to-do list
  • Turn off distractions
  • Reflect on what matters today

🩺 Surviving

Stabilize the basics and regain control.

  • Drink water or eat something nourishing
  • Cancel something non-essential
  • Ask for help or support
  • Take a break to rest
