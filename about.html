About The Reset Compass

The Reset Compass helps you quickly choose a short, intentional activity to reset your focus, energy, or mood. Wedges represent different wellness modes. Each mode suggests short activities you can do immediately.

How to use

  1. Select how you feel: Tap a wedge inside the compass or pick a Mode Card below.
  2. Choose an activity: In the Mode dialog, tap an activity to select it (it will highlight). You can add optional notes if you want.
  3. Complete your selection: Tap "Complete Selected" to record the activity. This updates your daily streak and opens your History.
  4. Daily limit: Only one Mode completion is allowed per day, but Quick Wins have no limit—use them anytime!

Privacy & Data

We respect your privacy. The Reset Compass stores only minimal data locally on your device (your reset history, streak, theme preference, and completion dates). We do not collect or transmit personal wellness data to external servers. Reviews and feedback you optionally submit may be stored on our servers. You can clear your local data at any time from the History dialog. For full details, see our Privacy Policy in the app menu.
