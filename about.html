About The Reset Compass

The Reset Compass helps you quickly choose a short, intentional activity to reset your focus, energy, or mood. Wedges represent different wellness modes. Each mode suggests short activities you can do immediately.

How to use

  1. Tap a wedge inside the compass or pick a Mode Card below.
  2. In the Mode dialog, select an activity (it highlights). Add optional notes if you want.
  3. Tap "Complete Selected" to record the selected activity; that updates your daily streak and opens History.
  4. Only one Mode completion is allowed per day; Quick Wins have no daily limit.

Privacy & Data

We respect your privacy. The Reset Compass stores only minimal data locally (your reset history, streak, theme preference, and the day you completed your mode). We do not collect or transmit personal data to external servers. You can clear your history at any time from the History dialog.
