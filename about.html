About The Reset Compass

The Reset Compass was created by Evolution of Wellness to help people quickly choose a short, intentional activity to reset their focus, energy or mood. Each direction represents a different wellness "mode" with a set of recommended quick wins.

Learn more about Evolution of Wellness at evolutionofwellness.com .

How to use

  1. Pick a direction around the compass or choose a mode card below the compass.
  2. See recommended quick wins in the modal that opens.
  3. Tap "Begin Activity" to record the activity into your history.

Your theme (light/dark) preference is saved. Mode colors are applied as translucent accents so they flow well with the app background.
