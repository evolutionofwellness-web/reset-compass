About The Reset Compass

The Reset Compass helps you pick a short activity to reset your focus, energy, or mood. The compass wedges show different wellness modes. Each mode has activities you can do right away.

How to use

  1. Pick how you feel: Tap a wedge on the compass or pick a Mode Card below.
  2. Pick an activity: In the Mode dialog, tap an activity to pick it (it will highlight). You can add notes if you want.
  3. Complete your pick: Tap "Complete Selected" to save the activity. This updates your daily streak and opens your History.

Privacy & Data

We respect your privacy. The Reset Compass stores minimal data on your device (your history, streak, theme, and dates). We do not collect or send your personal wellness data to servers. Reviews and feedback you submit may be stored on our servers. You can clear your data anytime from the History dialog. For full details, see our Privacy Policy in the app menu.
